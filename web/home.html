About Us

Welcome to the official banking system of Leo Club Tunis Ennasr City, a non-profit organization dedicated to serving our community and making a positive impact. Our club is part of the global Leo movement, empowering young leaders to create change through service projects and volunteer work.

This platform is designed to manage our club's finances, including member contributions, action funds, and financial dashboards. Together, we can achieve our goals and continue making a difference!
